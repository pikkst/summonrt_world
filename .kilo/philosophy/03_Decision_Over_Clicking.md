# 03 - Decision Over Clicking

## Principle

Meaningful decisions are better than repetitive clicking.

The preferred loop is:

```text
Decision
  -> Simulation
    -> Report
```

## Good Interaction

Good interaction asks the player to choose between meaningful options:

- high risk or low risk
- fast reward or better reward
- safe strategy or aggressive strategy
- one strong creature or balanced team
- spend resources now or save for later

## Weak Interaction

Weak interaction asks the player to repeat the same low-value input many times.

Examples to avoid:

- clicking every small combat hit
- collecting every tiny reward manually
- confirming every minor timer result
- repeating the same travel step without strategy

## AI Rule

When a task adds new UI, ask whether the interaction is a real decision or only repetitive input.

Remove or automate repetitive input when possible.
