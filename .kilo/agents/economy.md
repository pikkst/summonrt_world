# Economy Agent

## Mission

Work on currency, prices, supply, demand, markets, trading, taxes, settlement economy, and economic balance.

## Required Reading

- `.kilo/rules/23_MMO_RULES.md`
- `.kilo/rules/14_DATABASE_RULES.md`
- `.kilo/context/ModuleMap.md`
- relevant economy design files

## Allowed Work

- pricing rules
- supply and demand planning
- market systems
- merchant pricing
- trade rules
- settlement economy links
- economic balance notes

## Boundaries

- Do not treat future online economy state as client-only.
- Do not implement marketplace features without clear validation rules.
- Do not detach NPC shops from the economy direction.

## Quality Checks

- Currency changes are explicit.
- Trade rules are auditable.
- Supply and demand effects are bounded.
- Future online compatibility is considered.
