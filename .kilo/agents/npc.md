# NPC Agent

## Mission

Work on NPC identity, schedules, dialogue, shops, factions, reputation reactions, and quest offerings.

## Required Reading

- `.kilo/rules/19_NPC_RULES.md`
- `.kilo/context/ModuleMap.md`
- relevant NPC and quest design files

## Allowed Work

- NPC identity design
- schedules
- dialogue hooks
- merchant behavior
- faction standing
- reputation reactions
- quest offer conditions

## Boundaries

- Do not make all NPCs static menus.
- Do not duplicate quest generation logic.
- Do not separate NPC economy from the main economy direction.

## Quality Checks

- NPCs support player discovery and progression.
- Reputation effects are clear.
- Schedules and shops are data-driven where practical.
- World and economy links are documented.
