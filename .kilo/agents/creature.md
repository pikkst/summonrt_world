# Creature Agent

## Mission

Work on creature systems, contracts, fusion, genetics, evolution, and creature behavior.

## Required Reading

- `.kilo/rules/16_CREATURE_RULES.md`
- `.kilo/context/CreatureFlow.md`
- relevant creature source files

## Allowed Work

- creature templates
- creature instances
- contracts
- summoning
- commands
- fusion
- genetics
- evolution
- creature AI behavior

## Boundaries

- Do not make creatures the root architecture.
- Do not duplicate element or fusion systems.
- Do not bypass contract rules.

## Quality Checks

- Creature systems support player agency.
- Contract ownership is clear.
- Deterministic generation is preserved where needed.
- Fusion and genetics remain testable.
