## Implementation Details

### Trap Rooms
- **Generator**: `generateTrapInteraction(rng)` returns 3 trap variants (dart trap, toxic gas, hidden blade)
- **Resolution**: `resolveTrapRoom(choice, playerStats, rng)` - choices: dodge/duck/hold_breath/backflip/rush (agility check), block/parry (defense check), disarm (skill check), endure (take full damage)
- **Damage**: 8-12 damage base, reduced on successful defense

### Puzzle Rooms
- **Generator**: `generatePuzzleInteraction(rng, worldIndex)` returns 3 puzzle variants (elemental pedestals, rune sequence, celestial lock)
- **Resolution**: `resolvePuzzleRoom(choice, rng)` - 35% chance to succeed even on wrong answer, specific correct answers for each puzzle type
- **Scaling**: Higher world index adds flavor text for increased difficulty perception

### Elite Rooms
- **Generator**: `generateEliteInteraction(rng, worldIndex)` creates elite guardians with `enemyName` (e.g., "Alpha Warden") and scaled `enemyLevel`
- **Behavior**: Launches combat with elite creature; higher level = stronger stats

### Vendor Rooms
- **Generator**: `generateVendorInteraction(rng, worldIndex)` offers wandering merchant/peddler/forgotten trader with various wares (healing herbs, mana crystals, rations, soul crystals)
- **Resolution**: `resolveVendorRoom(itemId)` - removes gold, adds item to inventory
- **Stock**: Scaled by tier (1-20 items) with world-index-based restocking

### Treasure Rooms
- **Generator**: `generateTreasureInteraction(rng, worldIndex)` - mythical egg chance decays from 1% (world 1) to 0.1% (world 10+)
- **Resolution**: `resolveTreasureRoom()` - grants treasure loot, clears encounter
- **Rare Drop**: Mythical egg visually indicated with special highlighting

### UI Integration
Room interactions display in CombatPanel when `combat.roomInteraction?.active` is true:
- **Trap/Puzzle**: Grid of choice buttons with labels and descriptions
- **Vendor**: Item shop grid with prices and stock counts
- **Treasure**: Central claim button with rare drop notification
