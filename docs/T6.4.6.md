# Sprint 6 - T6.4.6: Add Boss Floor Generation Rules

## Objective
Add dedicated boss floor generation rules for world dungeon towers.

Required behavior:
- The final floor of each world contains a boss arena.
- The arena layout is open and non-maze-like.
- Environmental hazards are selected from the world element.
- Boss scaling follows the GDD formula.

## What Was Found

### Existing Dungeon Foundation
The dungeon system already had these completed foundations:

- `generateDungeonFloor(worldIndex, floorIndex, globalSeed)` creates deterministic maze floors.
- `generateDungeonTower(worldIndex, globalSeed)` builds the vertical tower and links floor exits to the next entrance.
- Room typing already supports entrance, boss, treasure, combat, trap, puzzle, rest, elite, and vendor rooms.
- Pathfinding utilities already verify reachability and shortest paths.

### Relevant Design Rules
`SummonerWorld_GDD.md` defines:

- Floors before the boss are procedurally generated.
- The final progression floor is the World Boss Arena.
- Boss HP scales as `BaseBossHP * (1 + (WorldIndex - 1) * 0.25)`.
- Bosses gain one new signature ability every 10 worlds.
- Environmental hazards appear during boss encounters.

`docs/Sumoner_World_dec/15_Dungeon_Bible.md` also defines that every world tower has a final boss arena and that dungeon generation must stay deterministic.

## What Was Done

### Added Boss Arena Types
Updated `summoner-world/src/types/game.ts` with:

- `DungeonFloorLayoutType`
- `DungeonEnvironmentalHazard`
- `DungeonBossScaling`

`DungeonFloorGraph` now optionally records:

- `layoutType`
- `isBossFloor`
- `worldElement`
- `environmentalHazards`
- `bossScaling`

### Added Boss Floor Generation
Updated `summoner-world/src/core/dungeonGenerator.ts` with:

- `BASE_BOSS_HP`
- `generateBossFloor(worldIndex, floorIndex, globalSeed)`
- `getWorldElement(worldIndex)`
- `getBossFloorEnvironmentalHazards(element)`
- `calculateBossScaling(worldIndex, baseBossHp)`

The boss floor generator creates a deterministic 5x5 open arena instead of a maze. The arena has:

- A fixed entrance at `arena_2_0`.
- A fixed boss room at `arena_2_2`.
- Fully open cardinal grid connections.
- Hazard rooms around the arena.
- No treasure rooms, because this floor is dedicated to the boss encounter.

### Integrated Boss Floors Into Towers
`generateDungeonTower()` now uses regular maze floors until the final floor, then replaces the final floor with `generateBossFloor()`.

If the final floor index is also a safe-floor interval, boss-floor behavior wins. This prevents a final boss arena from being converted into a safe rest/vendor floor.

### Added Boss Hazards
Hazards are selected from the deterministic world element cycle:

- Fire -> Lava Burst
- Water -> Tidal Surge
- Earth -> Seismic Fracture
- Air -> Cyclone Crosswind
- Lightning -> Storm Pulse
- Iron -> Magnetic Crush
- Nature -> Thorn Overgrowth
- Ice -> Frost Spikes
- Light -> Radiant Judgment
- Darkness -> Umbral Zone

Quest-only or special elements also have fallback hazard definitions for future use.

### Added Boss Scaling
Boss scaling now records:

- `baseBossHp`
- `hpMultiplier`
- `scaledBossHp`
- `signatureAbilityCount`

The formula matches the GDD:

```text
Boss HP = BaseBossHP * (1 + (WorldIndex - 1) * 0.25)
```

Signature abilities are counted as one new ability every 10 worlds.

### Added Tests
Updated `summoner-world/src/test/dungeonGenerator.test.ts` with coverage for:

- Final tower floor becomes a boss arena.
- Boss arena is open and non-maze-like.
- Boss arena entrance and boss room are reachable.
- Environmental hazards match the world element.
- Boss HP and signature ability scaling match the GDD.
- Boss floor generation is deterministic.
